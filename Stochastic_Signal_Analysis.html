Master Notation & Theoretical Reference

I. Probability & Random Variables
Probability Density Function (PDF)
$$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_X(x)dx = 1, \quad P(a \le X \le b) = \int_a^b f_X(x)dx $$
Expectation (Mean)
$$ m_X = E[X] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x f_X(x)dx $$
Variance (AC Power)
$$ \sigma_X^2 = E[(X-m_X)^2] = E[X^2] - m_X^2 $$
Characteristic Function
$$ \Phi_X(\omega) = E[e^{j\omega X}] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_X(x)e^{j\omega x}dx $$
II. Joint Statistics (Two Variables)
Joint Expectation (Correlation)
$$ E[XY] = R_{XY} = \iint xy f_{XY}(x,y) dx dy $$
Covariance
$$ C_{XY} = E[(X-m_X)(Y-m_Y)] = R_{XY} - m_X m_Y $$
Correlation Coefficient
$$ r_{XY} = \frac{C_{XY}}{\sigma_X \sigma_Y}, \quad -1 \le r \le 1 $$
Independence Condition
$$ f_{XY}(x,y) = f_X(x)f_Y(y) \implies E[XY] = E[X]E[Y] $$
III. Stochastic Processes (Time)
Mean Function
$$ m_\xi(t) = E[\xi(t)] $$
Autocorrelation Function (ACF)
$$ R_{\xi\xi}(t_1, t_2) = E[\xi(t_1)\xi^*(t_2)] $$
Wide-Sense Stationarity (WSS)
$$ 1.\ m_\xi(t) = \text{const} \quad 2.\ R_{\xi\xi}(t, t+\tau) = R_{\xi\xi}(\tau) $$
Total Average Power
$$ P_{avg} = R_{\xi\xi}(0) = E[|\xi(t)|^2] = \sigma_\xi^2 + |m_\xi|^2 $$
IV. Spectral Analysis
Wiener-Khinchin Theorem
$$ S_{XX}(f) = \mathcal{F}\{R_{XX}(\tau)\} = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} R_{XX}(\tau)e^{-j2\pi f \tau}d\tau $$
Properties of PSD
$$ S_{XX}(f) \ge 0, \quad S_{XX}(f) = S_{XX}(-f) \ (\text{real signals}) $$
Power from PSD
$$ P_{avg} = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} S_{XX}(f) df = R_{XX}(0) $$
White Noise
$$ S_{NN}(f) = \frac{N_0}{2}, \quad R_{NN}(\tau) = \frac{N_0}{2}\delta(\tau) $$
V. LTI Systems Response
Input-Output PSD Relation
$$ S_{YY}(f) = |H(f)|^2 S_{XX}(f) $$
Input-Output Cross-Correlation
$$ R_{YX}(\tau) = h(\tau) * R_{XX}(\tau) $$
Output Mean
$$ m_Y = m_X \cdot H(0) $$
VI. Ergodicity
Ergodicity in the Mean
$$ \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{1}{2T} \int_{-T}^{T} \xi(t) dt = E[\xi] $$
Ergodicity in Autocorrelation
$$ \lim_{T \to \infty} \frac{1}{2T} \int_{-T}^{T} \xi(t)\xi(t+\tau) dt = R_{\xi\xi}(\tau) $$
